Privacy Policy for Socials & Entertainment Blocker

This Privacy Policy outlines how Socials & Entertainment Blocker ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects the information obtained from users ("you") when using our Chrome extension. Our primary purpose is to help users focus by blocking distracting websites, and we are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We only collect the minimum amount of information needed to provide the functionality of the extension. Specifically, we use the webNavigation API to monitor and block access to specific websites. No web history or personally identifiable information is stored or shared. All processing happens locally within your browser.

Data Usage

The information collected via the webNavigation API is only used to prevent access to distracting websites in real-time. This data is not stored, transmitted, or shared with any third parties. It is used solely to enhance your productivity and focus by blocking certain websites.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your information to third parties. The data used by this extension remains strictly within your local browser environment.

User Rights

You can uninstall or disable the extension at any time via the Chrome browser's settings. Once uninstalled, the extension will no longer have any access to your browser or browsing behavior.

Security

We prioritize security and do not store any data outside your browser. The extension operates locally and does not transmit data over the internet. Your browsing habits and any data collected remain private and secure within your local environment.
